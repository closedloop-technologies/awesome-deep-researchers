# You.com Research Result

In 2026, deep research agents have shifted from simple summarization to autonomous, multi-step investigation protocols.

### **Top Models & Frameworks**
*   **Frontier Leaders:** GLM-5.1, Qwen 3.7, and DeepSeek V4 Pro lead on reasoning and coding benchmarks [[1]].
*   **Frameworks:** JAX (high-performance research), PyTorch, and TensorFlow remain standards, while MCP (Model Context Protocol) is the essential standard for connecting agents to internal data and tools [[2]].

### **Actionable Best Practices**
1.  **Iterative Planning:** Edit research plans before execution—define constraints, specific trusted sources, and target angles to prevent scope creep.
2.  **Protocol:** Adopt an **Expansion-Recursion-Synthesis** pattern: broaden search, dive into anomalies/contradictions, and synthesize into executive summaries with high/low confidence ratings.
3.  **Human-in-the-Loop:** Use agents for rapid contextualization (first-pass data gathering) but maintain human oversight for final synthesis and interpretation.
4.  **Tools:** Pair **Perplexity** (web discovery) with **Elicit/Consensus** (academic rigor) and **ChatGPT Deep Research** (complex agentic workflows).
